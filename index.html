Global Startup Awards SAARC, focuses on celebrating the spirit of entrepreneurship and promote bridging boundaries using innovation in our region comprising of Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka by appreciating them with our most coveted awards program which will be attended by the top notch entrepreneurs, corporates, investors and ecosystem builders from Nordic, Central Europe, SAARC and ASEAN.

Our Global Cross Pollination Panel comprising of our alumni network of mentors, entrepreneurs, advisers, ambassadors and Jury from some of the most coveted startups/institutions will help enable your idea to come to reality, scale up to other countries and maybe even get funded.

"Come celebrate entrepreneurship and let's bridge boundaries using innovation."
